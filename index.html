How It Works

THE BOUNCER IS ALWAYS ON THE DOOR

TrackRSmackR runs a silent, local policy engine that intercepts every cookie before it settles — and decides its fate in milliseconds.

01

Cookie Arrives

The moment a cookie tries to land, the Cookie Observer intercepts it before it touches storage.

02

Classified by Policy

The local policy engine reads it, tags it — essential, unknown, or flagged — in real time.

03

Locked in the Jar

Non-essential cookies get sealed in the encrypted Cookie Jar. They sit. They wait. They decay.

04

Smacked on a Timer

Timed decay cycles fire automatically. The tracker dies. No trace. No comeback.

05

You Stay Free

The Intelligence Snapshot shows you exactly what happened. Full transparency. Full control.
